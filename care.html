Care: Equipment and Accessories

When bringing a new dog home, it's best to feel confident that you have access to all of the supplies your furry friend might need. But figuring out where to begin can be challenging. That's why we're here to help!



Food & Nutrition

Necessities include:
Food bowl
Water bowl
Canned and/or dry food
Treats (cookies, bones, etc.)

Where you can buy these items:
Amazon
PetSmart
Chewy
Bark Box



Exercise & Outdoors

Necessities include:
Leash (4-6 feet)
Harness and/or collar
ID tag
Waste bags

Where you can buy these items:
Chewy
RuffWear
Petco
Doggie Walk Bags



Hygiene & Cleanliness

Necessities include:
Shampoo & conditioner
Nail clippers
Brush and/or comb
Toothbrush & toothpaste

Where you can buy these items:
Skout's Honor
Chewy
Amazon
I Heart Dogs



Housewarming & Comfort

Necessities include:
Dog bed
Absorbent training pads
First-aid supplies
Toys & puzzles

Where you can buy these items:
Chewy
Petco
Baxter Boo
Amazon



Think you have everything? Download Barking Royalty's checklist to see how you did!


Please note: There are a number of truly fantastic resources out there, many of which we haven't mentioned here! We'd highly recommend that you do a bit of extra searching to ensure that the items you purchase are ideal for your pup. And though it's not listed above, we cannot help but emphasize, yet again, our #1 piece of advice: talk to your vet!

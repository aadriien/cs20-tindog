We are TinDog.

Let us introduce ourselves.

We LOVE dogs.

And we're sure you do too. Here at TinDog, it is our goal to make both every dog and every owner happy. We are a group of programmers from Tufts University. With out web programming skills, we thought there would be nothing better than to utilize such skills to help all the lonely dogs and people out there. We hope you enjoy our service!

- Adrien Lynch, Ben McDermott, Alex Williams, and Lawrence Xu
